----- LOGIN -----

Command:
curl -X POST http://localhost:3000/auth/login -H "Content-Type: application/json" -d "{\"email\":\"your_email@example.com\",\"password\":\"password123\"}"

Output:
{"message":"OTP sent","loginSessionId":"z3z6wg"}


----- VERIFY OTP -----

Command:
curl -c cookies.txt -X POST http://localhost:3000/auth/verify-otp -H "Content-Type: application/json" -d "{\"loginSessionId\":\"z3z6wg\",\"otp\":\"591540\"}"

Output:
{"message":"OTP verified","sessionId":"yayp9te"}
